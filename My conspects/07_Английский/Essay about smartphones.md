Nowadays the idea of smartphone is not a new one. People use these communication tools all their time and completely everywhere. But do they realy make life better? Or are they just complicated toys that unnoticeably harm us?

To my mind, if you use your smartphone, it most likely will be searching for some new information. Early mobile phones were quite heavy and could only exchange information between two people now with some troubles. But today it is absolutely normal to explore completely new ideas or talk to anyone worldwide using just a small device in your pocket. Another benefit relates to modern services. Almost all businesses operate online today providing services easily accessible via phones. However, despite these conveniences, this constant digital connection has its hidden costs.

We can not ignore the downsides of using smartphones. First of all, mindless content consumption is extremly growing problem. People often find themselves scrolling TikTok or Instagram for hours, feeling exhausted after despite doing nothing productive at all. Also, scientific studies confirm that overusing mobile phones can lead to anxiety. The correlation for students is about 0.4 which is an incredibly high value for such common thing. And recognizing these negative effects is significantly important to find a balance in our digital lives.

All in all, nowadays it is unreal to live without smartphone. It has its advantages and disadvantages like any other technology. Since we can hardly imagine our life without mobile phones, we just have to teach ourselves mindful usage.

P.s just in case, the link to the paper: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/32903205
